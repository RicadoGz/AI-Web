# Student AI Portfolio Design

## Goal

Build a single-page personal website for Ricardo that feels cinematic, young, sharp, and product-minded. The homepage should create the impression that this is a student who is early to AI, explores new ideas seriously, and turns them into products worth paying attention to.

## Positioning

- Brand: `Ricardo — Exploring AI, Building Products`
- Tone: young, sharp, product-minded
- Priority order:
  1. product brain
  2. visual impact
  3. project credibility

## Page Structure

1. Hero with a cinematic background video
2. Four project cards in a unified visual system
3. Short "What I'm exploring" section
4. Low-key social footer
5. Chatbot placeholder in the bottom-right corner

No navigation bar should be present.

## Hero

- Keep the moving video as the emotional centerpiece
- Overlay only a small amount of foreground content
- Include:
  - brand label
  - hero title
  - one supporting sentence
  - one light AI/product signal line
- Copy:
  - Title: `I explore new AI ideas and turn them into products worth trying.`
  - Supporting sentence: `Still early, still learning, still building with AI every chance I get.`

## Visual Direction

- Palette: black + warm white + small amounts of blue
- Avoid purple cyberpunk and dashboard-heavy nerd aesthetics
- Motion should feel alive but controlled
- Use glass sparingly for chips, footer controls, and chatbot entry
- Keep the experience cinematic first, but let the product signal appear early

## Project Cards

- Show exactly four cards
- Use one shared card system, not four unrelated styles
- Each card includes:
  - project name
  - one-line summary
  - light meta tag(s)
  - outbound link target
- Cards should feel like AI product experiments, not resume bullets

## Supporting Sections

- Add a short "What I'm exploring" section with a few current themes
- Keep the footer social links low-key
- Add a chatbot placeholder only, not a working chatbot

## Implementation Notes

- The user will eventually swap the hero video for their own preferred motion asset
- The first screen should remain text-light so the video can lead
- Scrolling into projects should feel like moving from mood into proof
